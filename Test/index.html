Admission At Cubbies

Children cannot go to school until the age of 4 and hence it gets very challenging for parents to decide when the right time for enrolling their kid to a preschool program is.

Each child is unique so is their need and this makes it all the more difficult for parents to choose from the diverse programs available.

The very first thing to keep in mind when selecting the preschool program is the age of your child. We at cubbies have 4 different programs for different age groups to help your child learn better.

Next very important thing for you to keep in mind is the teachers and professionals that would be imparting this early education in your child's life . Early education plays a very fundamental role in deciding the overall personality of your child after he or she grows up and hence this is very important. We at cubbies have professionals who are best at their job and recognised all across the nation for their expertise.
